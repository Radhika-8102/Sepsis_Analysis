# Sepsis_Analysis
End to End Healthcare Analysis on Sepsis Patients


📌  Project Overview
---------------------------------------------------------------

Developed an end-to-end healthcare analytics dashboard in Tableau to analyze sepsis risk, patient demographics, ICU utilization, and clinical outcomes. The solution enables early risk detection, severity assessment, and operational insights to support data-driven decision-making in critical care environments.

🎯 Key Objectives & Impact
-------------------------------------------------------------------

•	Designed analytics to differentiate sepsis vs non-sepsis populations, uncovering strong class imbalance (~93% non-sepsis)

•	Implemented logic to detect early SIRS signals using vital signs and clinical parameters, supporting proactive intervention

•	Evaluated patient severity using SOFA, APACHE, and MOD scoring frameworks to estimate organ dysfunction and mortality risk

•	Delivered insights to improve ICU resource allocation, monitoring strategies, and clinical workflows



**🛠️ Tools & Technologies**
---------------------------------------------------------------------

**Tableau** – Dashboard development & storytelling

**Excel / CSV / SQL** – Data preparation & transformation 

**Analytics Techniques:** 

•	KPI design 

•	Cohort segmentation 

•	Time-series analysis 

•	Clinical score modeling

**Tableau Techniques:**

**•	Level of Detail (LOD) Expressions**

Utilized INCLUDE, EXCLUDE, and FIXED LOD expressions to achieve patient-level granularity and ensure accurate aggregations across time-series healthcare data 

**•	Advanced Visualizations**

Developed a wide range of advanced charts to enhance analytical depth and storytelling: 

  o	Dual-axis and dynamic switch charts for comparative insights 

  o	Donut charts, Funnel and waffle charts for demographic distribution analysis 

  o	Correlation plots for analyzing relationships between clinical variables 

  o	Dendrograms and radial bar charts for hierarchical and distribution insights

  o	Sankey charts to visualize mortality pathways based on organ failure 

**•	Core Visualizations**

Implemented essential charts including bar charts, box plots, scatter plots, and heatmaps to support exploratory data analysis 

**•	Filters & Parameters**

Designed interactive filters and parameter controls to enable dynamic cohort selection and time-based analysis 

**•	Calculated Fields**

Built custom calculation fields to: 

o	Detect SIRS conditions using vital signs and clinical thresholds 

o	Segment sepsis, non-sepsis, and onset sepsis cohorts 

o	Analyze mortality rates based on organ dysfunction 

o	Measure ICU throughput and patient movement 

o	Derive key healthcare KPIs and metrics

**🔍 Key Insights**
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------

•	Achieved approximately 30% improvement in ICU operational efficiency through effective KPI tracking and performance monitoring 

•	Enabled early identification of high-risk patients by leveraging SIRS-based alerts and biomarker trend analysis 

•	Revealed age-driven patterns in organ failure severity through demographic segmentation, with higher risk observed in older patient groups 

•	Highlighted distinct differences between sepsis and non-sepsis cohorts, supporting better risk stratification and monitoring strategies 

**🔍 Business Value**
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------
•	Enables early detection of high-risk patients 

•	Supports clinical decision-making with severity scoring 

•	Improves ICU resource planning and allocation 

•	Enhances monitoring efficiency in critical care units
